1161A 101st Legislative Session 1161
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 1161A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Representative Kull
1 An Act to amend provisions related to changing rooms, sleeping quarters, and
2 restrooms.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 20-13-58 be AMENDED:
5 20-13-58. Terms used in §§ 20-13-58 to 20-13-63, inclusive, mean:
6 (1) "Changing room," a room or area in which an individual may be in a state of
7 undress in the presence of others, including a multi-occupancy locker room or
8 shower room, which is open to the public;
9 (2) "Female," an individual who naturally has, had, will have, or would have had, but
10 for a congenital anomaly or intentional or unintentional disruption, the reproductive
11 system that produces, transports, and utilizes eggs for fertilization;
12 (3) "Male," an individual who naturally has, had, will have, or would have had, but for
13 a congenital anomaly or intentional or unintentional disruption, the reproductive
14 system that produces, transports, and utilizes sperm for fertilization;
15 (4) "Restroom," a room that includes at least one toilet or urinal;
16 (5) "Sleeping quarters," a room that contains a bed and in which more than one
17 individual is housed overnight;
18 (6) "Sex," an individual's biological sex, either male or female; and
19 (7) "Unisex or family room," a changing room or restroom that:
20 (a) Is intended for use by a single occupant, a single occupant and the
21 occupant's caregiver, or members of the same family;
22 (b) Is enclosed by means of floor-to-ceiling walls; and
23 (c) Is accessible by means of a full door, with a secure lock that prevents entry
24 by another individual while the room is in use.
25 Section 2. That § 20-13-59 be AMENDED:
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
1161A 2 1161
1 20-13-59. Except as otherwise provided in this section and §§ 13-32-22 and 13-
2 32-23, the state or a political subdivision of this state must designate, as being for the
3 exclusive use of females or males, any multi-occupancy changing room, multi-occupancy
4 restroom, or sleeping quarters in:
5 (1) A building or facility owned by the state or by a political subdivision of this state;
6 or
7 (2) A space leased to or occupied by the state or by a political subdivision of this state.
8 This section does not apply to any room used exclusively by firefighters, law
9 enforcement personnel, or musical and theatrical entertainers, or to any room used
10 entered for a governmental regulatory purpose.
11 Section 3. That § 20-13-61 be AMENDED:
12 20-13-61. The prohibitions set forth in § 20-13-60 do not apply to:
13 (1) An individual who accompanies a child under the age of ten, if the individual is the
14 child's parent or lawful chaperone;
15 (2) An individual who accompanies another individual requiring assistance because of
16 age or a disability;
17 (3) A law enforcement officer, fire official, or other state or county employee acting in
18 an official capacity;
19 (4) An individual providing emergency medical assistance;
20 (5) An individual providing custodial, maintenance, or inspection services, provided the
21 changing room or restroom is unoccupied; or
22 (6) An individual providing services or rendering aid:
23 (a) During a natural disaster or declared emergency; or
24 (b) When necessary to maintain order and safety.; or
25 (7) Firefighters, individuals who are actively engaged in musical, theatrical, or similar
26 performances, or law enforcement personnel, when using changing rooms, non-
27 public restrooms, or sleeping quarters designated for their exclusive use.
