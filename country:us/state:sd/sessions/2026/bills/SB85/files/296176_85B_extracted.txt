3 Section 1. That § 10-12-43 be AMENDED:
4 10-12-43. The governing body of the school district may raise additional revenues
5 for general fund purposes only, from property tax through the imposition of an excess tax
6 levy. The governing body of a school district may impose the excess tax levy with an
7 affirmative two-thirds vote of the governing body on or before July fifteenth of the year
8 prior to the year the taxes are payable. On any excess tax levy approved after July 1,
9 2002, the The governing body of the taxing district shall specify in the resolution the year
10 or number of years the excess tax levy will is to be applied and the total maximum dollar
11 amount of taxes payable over the full term of the excess tax levy.
12 The requirements for an announcement made pursuant to this section are as
13 follows:
14 (1) The decision of the governing body to originally impose or subsequently increase
15 an excess tax levy shall must be first published within ten days of the decision;
16 (2) Publication shall must be made at least twice in the legal newspaper designated
17 pursuant to § 13-8-10, with no fewer than five days between publication dates,
18 before the opt out takes effect;
19 (3) The announcement shall must be at least three newspaper columns in width and
20 four inches in length or at least one-sixth of a page in size, whichever size is
21 greater; and
22 (4) The announcement shall must be headed with the following statement in a typeface
23 no less than eighteen point eighteen-point type: "ATTENTION TAXPAYERS: NOTICE
24 OF PROPERTY TAX INCREASE OF $(fill in amount)." The remainder of the
25 announcement shall must consist of a reproduction of the "Resolution for Opt Out,"
