237B 101st Legislative Session 237
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 237B
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Senator Davis
This bill has been extensively amended (hoghoused) and may no longer be consistent with the original
intention of the sponsor.
1 An Act to establish additional notice and hearing requirements for applicants
2 seeking conditional use permits for proposed uses that materially affect
3 public infrastructure or rights-of wayrequire that counties provide written
4 notice to township supervisors of conditional use applications and appeals.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
6 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 11-2:
7 A county shall provide to each township supervisor who has an oath filed in the
8 office of the county auditor, pursuant to § 8-4-3, written notice of any application for the
9 conditional use of real property or any appeal filed pursuant to this chapter, for property
10 located in the township, at least ten days before the board of adjustment or other
11 approving authority holds a public hearing on the application or appeal.
