Section 1:
141A 101st Legislative Session 141
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 141A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Senator Foster
1 An Act to exempt certain sales at farmers' markets from sales tax.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 10-45:
4 There are specifically exempted from the provisions of this chapter and from the
5 computation of the amount of tax imposed by it, the gross receipts from the sale of any
6 fresh seasonal fruits and vegetables, meat, eggs, dairy, or baked goods, flowers, or
7 artisanal products, directly to consumers, at a farmers' market in this state.
8 For purposes of this section, "farmers' market" means a public, recurring
9 marketplace where multiple independent producers, primarily farmers, sell agricultural
10 products directly to consumers.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
