75A 101st Legislative Session 75
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDM ENT 75A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: The Chair of the Senate Committee on Appropriations at the request of the Bureau of Finance and
Management
1 An Act to amend the type of entities eligible to participate in the cybersecurity
2 services initiative.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That 2024 Session Laws, chapter 229, § 1 be AMENDED:
5 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated from the general fund the sum of
6 $7,000,000 to the Office of the Attorney General, for purposes the purpose of creating a
7 cybersecurity services initiative for counties and municipalities throughout the State of
8 South Dakota in this state.
9 Section 2. That 2024 Session Laws, chapter 229, § 2 be AMENDED:
10 Section 2. The funds moneys appropriated in section 1 of this Act must be used
11 exclusively to expand and improve cybersecurity for counties, nonprofit entities providing
12 drinking water and sanitary sewer utilities, utilities operated by a political subdivision, and
13 municipalities in the State of South Dakota, via any infrastructure and technology that can
14 protect the information technology assets ofcounties and municipalities in South Dakota
15 this state, and for administrative costs necessary for expending these dollars efficiently
16 and effectively.
17 Section 3. That 2024 Session Laws, chapter 229, § 3 be AMENDED:
18 Section 3. FundsMoneys must be disbursed according to the specific needs of the
19 project and in such a way that best advances the cybersecurity needs of the counties and
20 municipalities to be served state.
