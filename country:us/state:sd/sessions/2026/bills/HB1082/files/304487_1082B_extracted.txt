1082B 101st Legislative Session 1082
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 1082B
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Representative Wittman
1 An Act to establish parameters for the reimbursement of school districts that
2 provide free or reduced-price meals to students.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 13-35-2 be AMENDED:
5 13-35-2. Pupils and employed personnel of the school district or other agencies
6 contracting with the Department of Education, who are fed through a school food services
7 program may be charged for meals.
8 If a student is eligible for reduced-price meals through the National School Lunch
9 Program, 42 U.S.C. § 1751, et seq. (January 1, 2026), or the School Breakfast Program,
10 42 U.S.C. § 1773 (January 1, 2026), the board of a school district may not impose a
11 charge upon the student for any meals served through the National School Lunch Program
12 or the School Breakfast program, provided by the school district's food services program.
13 The board of a school district may impose a charge for any meal provided through
14 the school district's food services program to:
15 (1) Any student other than a student who is eligible for free or reduced-price meals
16 through the National School Lunch Program, 42 U.S.C. § 1751, et seq. (January 1,
17 2026), or the School Breakfast Program, (January 1, 2026);
18 (2) An employee of the school district; or
19 (3) Any other individual, not otherwise exempted in accordance with this section 2 of
20 this Act.
21 The Department of Education shall, with moneys appropriated in the general
22 appropriation bill, reimburse each school district for costs incurred by the district in
23 providing meals to students who are eligible for reduced-price meals in accordance with
24 this section, less any amount reimbursed through the National School Lunch Program or
25 the School Breakfast Program.
26 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 13-35:
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
1082B 2 1082
1 If a student is eligible for reduced-price meals through the National School Lunch
2 Program, 42 U.S.C. § 1751, et seq. (January 1, 2026), or the School Breakfast Program,
3 42 U.S.C. § 1773 (January 1, 2026), the board of a school district may not impose a
4 charge upon the student for any meals served by the district through the National School
5 Lunch Program or the School Breakfast Program.
6 Section 3. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 13-35:
7 On or before June thirtieth of each year, the department shall, based on
8 information forwarded by each school district, calculate the amount to be reimbursed by
9 the department for the cost of meals served to students who may not be charged,
10 pursuant to section 2 of this Act. The department shall:
11 (1) Subtract the amount to be reimbursed to the district for each meal served to a
12 student eligible for reduced-price meals through the National School Lunch
13 Program, 42 U.S.C. § 1751, et seq. (January 1, 2026), or the School Breakfast
14 Program, 42 U.S.C. § 1773 (January 1, 2026), from the amount reimbursed to the
15 district for a meal served to a student eligible for free meals through either
16 program; and
17 (2) Multiply the difference of subdivision (1) by the number of meals served to students
18 who may not be charged, pursuant to section 2 of this Act.
19 The Department of Education shall, with moneys appropriated in the general
20 appropriation bill, reimburse each school district in accordance with this section.
