Section 1:
1103A 101st Legislative Session 1103
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 1103A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Representative Odenbach
1 An Act to require that annual withdrawal of groundwater from any groundwater
2 source may not exceed the annual recharge of water to the groundwater
3 source.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. That § 46-6-3.1 be AMENDED:
6 46-6-3.1. No application to appropriate groundwater may be approved if,
7 according to the best information reasonably available, it is probable that the quantity of
8 water withdrawn annually from a groundwater source will exceed the quantity of the
9 average estimated annual recharge of water to the groundwater source. An application
10 may be approved, however, for withdrawals of groundwater from any groundwater
11 formation older than or stratigraphically lower than the greenhorn formation in excess of
12 the average estimated annual recharge for use by water distribution systems.
13 Section 2. This Act is effective beginning July 1, 2027.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
