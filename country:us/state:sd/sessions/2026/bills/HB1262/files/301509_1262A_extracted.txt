Section 1:
1262A 101st Legislative Session 1262
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 1262A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Representative Greenfield
1 An Act to make an appropriation for the construction of a juvenile corrections center
2 a non-residential, school-based, therapeutic services facility in Brown County
3 and to declare an emergency.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. There is appropriated $2,000,000 from the general fund, to the Department of
6 Corrections, to contract for the construction, completion, and equipping of a regional juvenile
7 corrections center in Brown County, including heating, plumbing, water, sewer, electric
8 facilities, architectural and engineering services, and other services or actions as may be
9 required Education for the purpose of providing a grant to an eligible nonprofit organization
10 to acquire, renovate, and equip a facility in which the organization may offer non-residential,
11 school-based, therapeutic services for students receiving instruction in an alternative setting.
12 Section 2. The administration of the design and construction of the project authorized in this
13 Act is under the general charge and supervision of the Bureau of Human Resources and
14 Administration, as provided in chapter 5-14.
15 Section 2. A nonprofit organization is eligible for the grant under section 1 of this Act only if
16 the organization demonstrates experience in operating a program that offers integrated
17 educational and therapeutic services, in partnership with school districts and other community
18 systems. The nonprofit organization may not use any moneys awarded as part of the grant
19 to provide residential treatment, inpatient services, or twenty-four-hour care for a student.
20 Section 3. The secretary of the Department of Corrections Education shall approve vouchers
21 and the state auditor shall draw warrants to pay expenditures authorized in this Act.
22 Section 4. Any amounts appropriated in this Act not lawfully expended or obligated shall
23 revert in accordance with the procedures prescribed in chapter 4-8.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
1262A 2 1262
1 Section 5. Whereas, this Act is necessary for the support of the state government and its
2 existing public institutions, an emergency is hereby declared to exist, and this Act shall be in
3 full force and effect from and after its passage and approval.
