14 § 13. Private property shall may not be taken for public use, or damaged, without

18 domain may not be exercised for the purpose of transferring private property to a non-
19 governmental entity solely to promote economic development or increase tax revenue
20 without the provision of a public use. A benefit which that may accrue to the owner as the
21 result of an improvement made by any private corporation shall may not be considered in
22 fixing the compensation for property taken or damaged. The fee of land taken for railroad
23 tracks or other highways shall remain in such owners remains in the owner, subject to the
24 use for which it is taken. For purposes of this section, public use does not include an
25 increase in the tax base, tax revenues, employment, or general economic health.
