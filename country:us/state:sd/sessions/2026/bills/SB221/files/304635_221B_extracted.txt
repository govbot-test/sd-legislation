20 form of the chemical nicotine, including any salt or complex, from any source,
21 nicotinic alkaloids, nicotine analogs, and any substance intended to mimic or
22 replicate the pharmacological effect of nicotine. The term includes delivery
23 systems, components, parts, or accessories. The term does not include food, drugs,
