Section 1:
233A 101st Legislative Session 233
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 233A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Senator Davis
1 An Act to prohibit the assessment of certain administrative fees or penalties related
2 to the provision of care by an out-of-network provider.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 58-17F:
5 A health carrier may not, through contract or otherwise, assess or impose upon a
6 participating health care provider an administrative fee or, penalty, or payment reduction,
7 related to any provision of care to a patient that, member, or enrollee, which involved an
8 out-of-network provider.
9 For purposes of this section, an "out-of-network provider," means a provider, of
10 any item or service, who is not a participating provider.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
