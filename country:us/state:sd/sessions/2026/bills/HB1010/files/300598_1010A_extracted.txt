AMENDME NT 1010A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Representative Shorma
1 An Act to modify requirements regarding the use of a mobile electronic device while
2 operating a motor vehicle.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 32-26-47.1 be AMENDED:
5 32-26-47.1. No person may operate a motor vehicle while using a mobile
6 electronic device. A violation of this section is a Class 2 misdemeanor.
7 This section does not apply to:
8 (1) A law enforcement officer, firefighter, emergency medical technician, paramedic,
9 operator of an authorized emergency vehicle, or similarly engaged paid or
10 volunteer public safety first responder during the performance of that person's
11 official duties, and a public utility employee or contractor acting within the scope
12 of that person's employment;
13 (2) The use of a mobile electronic device for emergency the following purposes,
14 including:
15 (a) Using a text messaging device to contact a 911 system,;
16 (b) Placing an emergency call to a law enforcement agency, health care
17 provider, fire department, or other emergency services agency or entity, or
18 to report;
19 (c) Reporting to appropriate authorities a fire, traffic accident, serious road
20 hazard, or medical or hazardous materials emergency, or to report;
21 (d) Reporting the operator of another motor vehicle who is driving in a reckless
22 or otherwise unsafe manner, or who appears to be driving under the
23 influence of alcohol or drugs, or to report; or
24 (e) Reporting a crime;
25 (3) The use of a global positioning or navigation system feature of a mobile electronic
26 device, but does apply to provided the operator of the motor vehicle is not manually

1 entering information into the global positioning or navigation system feature of the
2 device;
3 (4) Reading, selecting, or entering a telephone number or name in a mobile electronic
4 device for the purpose of making or receiving a telephone call and using the device
5 for the call, or if a person otherwise activates or deactivates a feature or function
6 of a mobile electronic device; or
7 (5) The use of a mobile electronic device in a voice-operated or hands-free mode if the
8 operator of the motor vehicle does not use the operator's hands to operate the
9 device, except to tap or in some other manner, activate or deactivate a feature or
10 function of the device; or
11 (5) The use of a mobile electronic device held in the hand by an operator while driving
12 any type of agricultural equipment, excluding a truck, provided the agricultural
13 equipment is not designed to exceed twenty-five miles per hour.
