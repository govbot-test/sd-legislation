Section 1:
1222D 101st Legislative Session 1222
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 1222D
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Representative Lems
1 An Act to prohibit members of the Board of Economic Development from having any
2 a direct interest in moneys from or approved by the Board of Economic
3 Development.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 1-16G:
6 A member of the Board of Economic Development may not have an a direct
7 ownership interest in, or serve on the board of, any entity receiving an award,
8 disbursement, expenditure, or grant of public moneys from or approved by the Board of
9 Economic Development.
10 For purposes of this section, ownership of stock or another equity interest
11 purchased on the open market and held as a passive investment, without the ability to
12 control, influence, or direct the affairs of the entity, does not constitute direct ownership.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
