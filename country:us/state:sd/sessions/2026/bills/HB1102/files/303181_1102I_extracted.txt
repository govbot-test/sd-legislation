Section 1:
1102I 101st Legislative Session 1102
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 1102I
FOR THE HOUSE ENGROSSED BILL
Introduced by: Representative Baxter
1 An Act to reschedule the annual report filing date for a limited liability company and
2 a limited liability partnership filing certain annual reports.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 59-11-25 be AMENDED:
5 59-11-25. The Except as otherwise provided in this section, the first annual
6 reportshall of a filing entity or qualified foreign entity must be delivered to the Office of
7 the Secretary of State before the first day of the second month of the year following the
8 year in which a the filing entity or qualified foreign entity, except Except in the case of a
9 bank organized pursuant to § 51A-3-1.1 and a limited partnership organized pursuant to
10 chapter 48-7, was authorized to transact business. The Each subsequent annual report
11 shall must be delivered to the Office of the Secretary of State by the same date each
12 subsequent year.
13 The first annual report of a limited liability company or a limited liability
14 partnership, authorized to transact business during the month of January, must be
15 delivered to the Office of the Secretary of State on or before January thirty-first of the
16 ensuing year. In all other cases, the annual report required of a limited liability company
17 or a limited liability partnership must be delivered to the Office of the Secretary of State
18 on or before January thirty-first of each year any entity that is required by law to file an
19 annual report must do so:
20 (1) In the month representing the first-year anniversary of the entity's formation and
21 in each same month annually thereafter; or
22 (2) Beginning in the calendar year after the entity's formation, on or before January
23 thirty-first, and each January thirty-first thereafter.
24 At the time of formation, an entity shall indicate on the formation document which
25 filing date the entity has selected.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
1102I 2 1102
1 An entity that is in good standing with the secretary of state and observes the filing
2 date set in subdivision (1) may choose to observe the filing date set in subdivision (2) and
3 an entity that is in good standing with the secretary of state and observes the filing date
4 set in subdivision (2) may choose to observe the filing date set in subdivision (1), by
5 submitting to the secretary of state a change-of-filing-date form.
6 Section 2. This Act is effective beginning January 1, 2027.
7
