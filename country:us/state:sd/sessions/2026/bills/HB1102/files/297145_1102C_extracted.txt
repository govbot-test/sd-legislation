AMENDME NT 1102C
FOR THE HOUSE STATE AFFAIRS ENGROSSED BILL
Introduced by: Representative Baxter
1 An Act to reschedule the annual report filing date for a limited liability company and
2 a limited liability partnership.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 59-11-25 be AMENDED:
5 59-11-25. The Except as otherwise provided in this section, the first annual
6 reportshall of a filing entity or qualified foreign entity must be delivered to the Office of
7 the Secretary of State before the first day of the second month of the year following the
8 year in which a the filing entity or qualified foreign entity, except a bank organized
9 pursuant to § 51A-3-1.1 and limited partnership organized pursuant to chapter 48-7, was
10 authorized to transact business. The Each subsequent annual report shall must be
11 delivered to the Office of the Secretary of State by the same date each subsequent year.
12 The first annual report of a limited liability company or a limited liability
13 partnership, authorized to transact business during the month of January, must be
14 delivered to the Office of the Secretary of State on or before January thirty-first of the
15 ensuing year following the year in which the. In all other cases, the annual report required
16 of a limited liability company or a limited liability partnership was authorized to transact
17 business, and each January thirty-first thereafter must be delivered to the Office of the
18 Secretary of State on or before January thirty-first of each year.
19 Section 2. This Act is effective beginning January 1, 2027.
