Section 1:
1294C 101st Legislative Session 1294
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 1294C
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Representative Andera
1 An Act to prohibit geoengineering experimentation in this state.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 34A-1:
4 No person may engage in geoengineering experimentation in this state.
5 A violation of this section is a Class 6 felony.
6 For purposes of this section, "geoengineering" means the an intentional, large-
7 scale
8 intervention in the earth’s climate system to counteract climate change by:
9 (1) Removing carbon dioxide from the atmosphere; or
10 (2) Reflecting sunlight away from earth alteration or manipulation of the
11 environment, typically involving the release of aerosols, chemicals, chemical compounds,
12 electromagnetic radiation, or other physical agents, which increase air pollution and effect
13 changes to the earth's atmosphere or surface, including solar radiation modification,
14 sunlight reflection methods, climate intervention, stratospheric aerosol injection, cirrus
15 cloud thinning, cloud seeding, and carbon capture and storage.
16 The term does not include natural conservation activities designed to preserve or
17 restore forests, wetlands, grasslands, and other existing ecosystems.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
