Section 1:
127A 101st Legislative Session 127
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 127A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Senator Howard
1 An Act to limit nuisances caused by data centers.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 21-10:
4 Terms used in sections 1 to 4, inclusive, of this Act mean:
5 (1) "Data center," a centralized repository for the dissemination, management,
6 processing, and storage of electronic data and information, including:
7 (a) Application, cloud, or web hosting;
8 (b) Cloud computing;
9 (c) Colocation services;
10 (d) Cryptocurrency mining;
11 (e) Media streaming support;
12 (f) Platform as a service;
13 (g) Software as a service; and
14 (h) Any other comparable digital service. which has an anticipated peak electrical
15 demand of ten megawatts or greater; and
16 (2) "Residential area," any area designated or zoned for residential use pursuant to
17 local law or zoning ordinance.
18 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 21-10:
19 Effective July 1, 2026, a facility housing a data center may not be established within
20 one mile of any residence or residential area, but a county, municipality, or township may
21 adopt more restrictive data center siting requirements.
22 Section 3. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 21-10:
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
127A 2 127
1 A data center may not exceed a continuous noise level of forty-five decibels,
2 measured according to federal or local environmental standards, at the nearest property
3 line of a residential area.
4 Section 4. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 21-10:
5 A data center in violation of section 2 or 3 of this Act is declared a nuisance. The
6 state's attorney or any affected citizen may maintain an action against the operator of the
7 data center, and against the owner of the building in which or ground upon which the
8 nuisance exists, to enjoin the nuisance.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
