Section 1:
7A 101st Legislative Session 7
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDM ENT 7A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Senator Rohl
1 An Act to modify the tax rate on gold severed in this state, and to repeal an
2 additional tax on gold severed in this state.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 10-39-43 be AMENDED:
5 10-39-43. For the privilege of severing precious metals in this state, there There
6 is imposed on each person severing gold in this state, a severance tax of four dollars per
7 ounce of one percent of the market value of gold severed by the person in this state.
8 For purposes of this section, "market value" means the average London Bullion
9 Market Association price of gold, over the calendar quarter in which the gold was severed.
10 Section 2. That § 10-39-68 be REPEALED.
11 In addition to the tax imposed in § 10-39-43, there is hereby imposed an additional
12 tax on each ounce of precious metals severed as follows:
13 (1) Four dollars per ounce of gold severed during a quarter if the average price of the
14 precious metal is eight hundred dollars per ounce or greater;
15 (2) Three dollars per ounce of gold severed during a quarter if the average price of the
16 precious metal is seven hundred dollars per ounce or greater;
17 (3) Two dollars per ounce of gold severed during a quarter if the average price of the
18 precious metal is six hundred dollars per ounce or greater; and
19 (4) One dollar per ounce of gold severed during a quarter if the average price of the
20 precious metal is five hundred dollars per ounce or greater.
21 The average price of gold shall be computed by summing the daily London Final
22 price of the precious metal for each business day of the quarter and dividing by the number
23 of business days in the quarter.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
