Section 1:
121C 101st Legislative Session 121
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 121C
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Senator Otten
This bill has been extensively amended (hoghoused) and may no longer be consistent with the original
intention of the sponsor.
1 An Act to make an appropriation for developing the economic viability of South
2 Dakotatransfer moneys and make an appropriation for the replacement of the
3 Richmond Lake dam and spillway and to declare an emergency.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. The state treasurer shall transfer $101 from the trust fund for unclaimed property,
6 created in § 43-41B-24.4, to the general fund.
7 Section 2. There is appropriated $101 from the general fund, to the Office of the
8 Commissioner of School and Public Lands, for the replacement of the Richmond Lake dam and
9 spillway.
10 Section 3. The commissioner of school and public lands shall approve vouchers and the state
11 auditor shall draw warrants to pay expenditures authorized in this Act.
12 Section 4. Any amounts appropriated in this Act not lawfully expended or obligated shall
13 revert in accordance with the procedures prescribed in chapter 4-8.
14 Section 5. Whereas, this Act is necessary for the support of the state government and its
15 existing public institutions, an emergency is hereby declared to exist, and this Act shall be in
16 full force and effect from and after its passage and approval.
