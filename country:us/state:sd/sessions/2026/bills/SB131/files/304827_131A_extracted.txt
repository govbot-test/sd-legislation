2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 131A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Senator Zikmund
1 An Act to revise the season in which a nonprofit organization may host a special
2 pheasant hunt for disabled veterans and Purple Heart recipients.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 41-11-5.5 be AMENDED:
5 41-11-5.5. Any A nonprofit organization may establish host a special pheasant
6 hunt in which disabled veterans or and Purple Heart recipients of the United States Armed
7 Forces of the armed forces of the United States may participate. The special pheasant
8 hunt may be held before or during the regular pheasant during the private shooting
9 preserve season, pursuant to § 41-10-11. No fee may be charged to participants in any
10 such special pheasant the hunt, by either the state or the sponsoring organization.
11 The Game, Fish and Parks Commission shall promulgate rules, pursuant to chapter
12 1-26, to administer the special pheasant hunts authorized in this section.
13 For the purposes of this section a disabled veteran is a person who meets one of
14 the following criteria "disabled veteran" means an individual who is:
15 (1) A resident or nonresident veteran of the armed forces of the United States whohas:
16 (a) Has been adjudicated by the United States Department of Veterans Affairs as being
17 twenty percent or more disabled due to a service-connected disability or has
18 received the United States Department of Veterans Affairs K Award; or
19 (b) Is designated by the United States Department of Veterans Affairs to receive
20 Special Monthly Compensation Level K pursuant to 38 U.S.C. § 1114 (January 1,
21 2026); or
22 (2) A resident of this state who has served on active duty in the armed forces of the
23 United States or has served as a member of the armed forces reserve or national
24 guard, and the resident is forty percent or more disabled for the purposes of
25 receiving social security benefits been adjudicated by the United States
