Section 1:
1187A 101st Legislative Session 1187
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 1187A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Representative Fitzgerald
1 An Act to add coaches to the list of mandatory reporters of child abuse or neglect.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 26-8A-3 be AMENDED:
4 26-8A-3. Any physician, dentist, doctor of osteopathy, chiropractor, optometrist,
5 emergency medical technician, paramedic, mental health professional or counselor,
6 podiatrist, psychologist, religious healing practitioner, social worker, hospital intern or
7 resident, parole or court services officer, law enforcement officer, coach who is subject to
8 the requirement of § 13-36-10, teacher, school counselor, school official, nurse, licensed
9 or registered child welfare provider, employee or volunteer of a domestic abuse shelter,
10 employee or volunteer of a child advocacy organization or child welfare service provider,
11 chemical dependency counselor, coroner, dental hygienist, or any safety-sensitive position
12 as defined in § 3-6C-1, who has reasonable cause to suspect that a child under the age of
13 eighteen has been abused or neglected, as defined in § 26-8A-2, shall report that
14 information in accordance with §§ 26-8A-6, 26-8A-7, and 26-8A-8. Any person who
15 intentionally fails to make the required report is guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor. Any
16 person who knows or has reason to suspect that a child has been abused or neglected as
17 defined in § 26-8A-2 may report that information as provided in § 26-8A-8.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
