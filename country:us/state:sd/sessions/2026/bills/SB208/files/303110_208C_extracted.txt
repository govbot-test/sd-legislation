Section 1:
208C 101st Legislative Session 208
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 208C
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Senator Hulse
1 An Act to require the award of certain disbursements to prevailing owners and
2 taxpayers in appeals of property classifications or assessments.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 10-11-45.1 be AMENDED:
5 10-11-45.1. The circuit court may award disbursements, including reasonable
6 attorneys' attorney fees, in an action brought to circuit court pursuant to this chapter by
7 any appellant relative to the classification or amount of assessment of property, if the
8 appellant does not prevail in its the appeal of the property classification or amount of
9 assessment.
10 If an aggrieved owner or taxpayer prevails in the appeal reclassification of the
11 property or obtains an assessment resulting in a reduction of the appealed assessment by
12 at least twenty percent, the court must award disbursements and reasonable attorney
13 fees to the owner or taxpayer.
14 Section 2. That § 10-11-78 be AMENDED:
15 10-11-78. On motion, the Supreme Court may award reasonable attorneys' fees
16 in In an action brought to the Supreme Court of South Dakota, pursuant to this chapter,
17 against any appellant relative to the classification or amount of assessment of property,
18 the court, upon motion:
19 (1) May award disbursements, including reasonable attorney fees, if the appellant does
20 not prevail in its the appeal of the reclassification of the property or obtain an
21 assessment. The motion shall be accompanied by counsel's that is lower than the
22 appealed assessment; or
23 (2) Must award disbursements, including reasonable attorney fees to any aggrieved
24 owner or taxpayer, to an appellant that prevails in the appeal reclassification of the
