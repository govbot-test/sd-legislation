1 A RESOLUTION recognizing the 250th anniversary of the Declaration of
2 Independence.
3 WHEREAS, July 4, 2026, will signify 250 years since the founding fathers voted to finalize
4 the text of the Declaration of Independence, which established the sovereignty of the original
5 thirteen colonies; and
6 WHEREAS, indigenous nations, generations of immigrants, civil rights advocates, and
7 countless others generations of natural-born Americans and immigrants have worked
8 tirelessly to promote and protect freedom and justice for all, and to create, based on those
9 principles, the United States we recognize today; and
10 WHEREAS, the tribal nations within this state's borders, as well as this state's pioneer
11 heritage, agricultural innovations, and its resilient civil spirit contributed to the growth of this
12 incredible nation and will continue to support the United States of America for many years yet
13 to come; and
14 WHEREAS, the 250th anniversary of the Declaration of Independence presents numerous
15 opportunities to educate all Americans about our nation, to celebrate our republican
16 institutions, and to renew a shared commitment to ensuring a resilient republic Constitutional
17 Republic for all;
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, by the Senate of the One Hundred First Legislature
19 of the State of South Dakota, that the 250th anniversary of the Declaration of Independence
20 be recognized as a time to reflect upon the founding principles of ordered liberty, democracy,
21 and equality, and natural rights; to celebrate the profound achievements of our first 250
22 years; and to prepare for all that this great nation has yet to experience during the next 250
23 years.
