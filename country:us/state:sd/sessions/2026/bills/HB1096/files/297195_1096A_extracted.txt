1 (c) Subsections 48-7A-306(c) and 48-7A-307(b) of the Uniform Partnership Act
2 apply to both general and limited partners of a limited liability limited partnership. Section
3 § 48-7-303 also applies to limited partners. Upon qualification or formation, the entity is
4 a limited partnership and a limited liability limited partnership for all purposes of this
5 chapter.
6 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 48-7:
7 A limited liability limited partnership may be formed by delivering to the secretary
8 of state a certificate of limited liability limited partnership. Delivery may be made by
9 electronic transmission, if and to the extent permitted by the secretary of state. If the
10 certificate is filed in typewritten or printed form and not transmitted electronically, the
11 secretary of state may require one exact or conformed copy to be delivered with the
12 certificate.
13 The certificate must contain:
14 (1) The name of the limited liability limited partnership, which has as the last words or
15 letters of its name the words "Registered Limited Liability Limited Partnership," the
16 abbreviation "L.L.L.P.," or the designation "LLLP";
17 (2) The street address of the limited liability limited partnership's chief executive office
18 and, if different, the street address of an office in this state, if any;
19 (3) If the limited liability limited partnership does not have an office in this state, the
20 information required pursuant to § 59-11-6;
21 (4) A statement that the entity elects to be a limited liability limited partnership; and
22 (5) A deferred effective date, if any.
23 A limited liability limited partnership is formed when the certificate is filed with the
24 secretary of state or on a deferred effective date thereafter.
25 Section 3. This Act is effective beginning January 1, 2027.
