Section 1:
1080A 101st Legislative Session 1080
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 1080A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Representative Reisch
1 An Act to allow a business owner who is a veteran to display military specialty plates
2 on a noncommercial vehicle registered to the veteran's business and to limit
3 personal liability to the business owner and veteran.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 32-5:
6 Any individual who is a business owner, and a veteran as defined in § 33A-2-1,
7 and who meets the requirements of §§ 32-5-154 to 32-5-166, inclusive, may apply for
8 and display one set of military specialty plates on a noncommercial vehicle registered to
9 the business, provided the veteran is listed as an additional owner on the certificate of
10 title of the vehicle pursuant to § 32-3-18.
11 The vehicle registration must state each owner of the motor vehicle pursuant to
12 §§ 32-5-2 and 32-5-3.
13 The vehicle must be a noncommercial automobile, pickup truck, or van licensed
14 pursuant to § 32-5-6.
15 Pursuant to § 32-5-159, the owner must pay an additional fee of ten dollars for the
16 initial issuance of military specialty plates, in addition to noncommercial annual
17 registration and plate fees, unless the owner is exempt from fees pursuant to § 32-5-160.
18 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 32-5:
19 A business owner and a veteran, as defined in § 33A-2-1, who is listed as an
20 additional owner on the title and registration of a vehicle registered to the veteran's
21 business, pursuant to section 1 of this Act, is not personally liable for any damages from
22 an accident involving a vehicle registered to the business does not assume any additional
23 liability resulting from the ownership of military special plates.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
