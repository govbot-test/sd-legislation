Section 1:
1038C 101st Legislative Session 1038
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 1038C
FOR THE HOUSE COMMERCE AND ENERGY ENGROSSED
BILL
Introduced by: The Chair of the Committee on Commerce and Energy at the request of the Public Utilities Commission
of the State of South Dakota
1 An Act to allow the Public Utilities Commission to assess actual costs to data centers
2 that are customers of public utilities.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 49-34A:
5 The commission may assess a customer of a public utility the actual costs
6 associated with processing a request for approval of a contract with deviations, or other
7 electric service agreement with a public utility, related to the provision of electric service
8 to a customer that is a data center with a peak demand of ten megawatts or greater.
9 The commission shall forward any moneys collected under this section to the state
10 treasurer, for deposit in the South Dakota Public Utilities Commission gross receipts tax
11 fund established in § 49-1A-2.
12 For the purposes of this section, "data center" means a centralized repository for
13 the primary purpose of storage, management, dissemination, and processing of electronic
14 data and information, including the mining of digital currency.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
