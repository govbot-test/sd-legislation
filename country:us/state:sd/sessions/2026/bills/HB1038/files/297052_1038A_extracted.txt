Section 1:
1038A 101st Legislative Session 1038
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 1038A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: The Chair of the Committee on Commerce and Energy at the request of the Public Utilities Commission
of the State of South Dakota
1 An Act to allow the Public Utilities Commission to assess actual costs to large new
2 customers data centers.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 49-34A:
5 The commission may assess a filing party the actual costs associated with
6 processing a docket brought pursuant to § 49-34A-56.
7 The commission may assess a customer the actual costs associated with processing
8 a request for approval of a contract related to the provision of electric service to a
9 customer approved, or eligible for approval, as a large new customer pursuant to § 49-
10 34A-56 with deviations, or other electric service agreement, related to the provision of
11 electric service to a customer that is a data center with a peak demand of ten megawatts
12 or greater.
13 The commission shall forward any moneys collected under this section to the state
14 treasurer, for deposit in the South Dakota Public Utilities Commission gross receipts tax
15 fund established in § 49-1A-2.
16 For the purposes of this section, "data center" means a centralized repository for
17 the storage, management, dissemination, and processing of electronic data and
18 information, including the mining of digital currency.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
