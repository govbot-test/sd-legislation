Section 1:
1172A 101st Legislative Session 1172
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 1172A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Representative Novstrup
1 An Act to terminate certain school district excess tax levies.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. An excess tax levy approved by a school district pursuant to § 10-12-43 or 10-
4 12-43.1, prior to July 1, 2002, may not be imposed in 2026 or any year thereafter an amount
5 not exceeding:
6 (1) For taxes payable in 2027, one hundred percent of the original excess tax levy
7 amount approved by the district;
8 (2) For taxes payable in 2028, fifty percent of the original excess tax levy amount
9 approved by the district; and
10 (3) For taxes payable in 2029 and each year thereafter, zero percent of the original
11 excess tax levy amount approved by the district.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
