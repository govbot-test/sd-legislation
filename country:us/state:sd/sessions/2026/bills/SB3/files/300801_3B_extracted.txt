Section 1:
3B 101st Legislative Session 3
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDM ENT 3B
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Senator Rohl
1 An Act to limit the types of residential improvements for which counties,
2 municipalities, and townships may require a permit.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 11-10:
5 Notwithstanding any authority granted to a county, municipality, or township to
6 prescribe building codes or standards or to issue building permits, a county, municipality,
7 or township may not require a building permit for:
8 (1) The repair or substantially similar replacement of any of the following existing
9 exterior parts of an owner-occupied residential structure:
10 (a) Door, if the dimensions remain the same;
11 (b) Downspout;
12 (c) Fencing panel or post;
13 (d) Gutter;
14 (e) Roof flashing, shingles, shakes, or tiles coverings and related
15 weatherproofing components;
16 (f) Siding, if nonstructural; and
17 (g) Window, if the dimensions remain the same; or.
18 (2) Any interior alteration, improvement, or renovation of an owner-occupied
19 residential structure, which does not affect the structure's:
20 (a) Air conditioning, electric, heating, plumbing, or ventilation system in a
21 manner requiring any load-bearing modification;
22 (b) Foundation;
23 (c) Load-bearing walls;
24 (d) Roof frame; or
25 (e) Support beams.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
3B 2 3
1 Nothing in this section relieves a homeowner from compliance with any other
2 applicable building code or ordinance.
3 A county, municipality, or township may require building permits for repairs
4 pursuant to this section or substantially similar replacements for exterior parts of any
5 designated historical property as authorized under Chapter 1-19B.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
