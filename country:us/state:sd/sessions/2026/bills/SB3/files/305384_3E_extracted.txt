Section 1:
3E 101st Legislative Session 3
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDM ENT 3E
FOR THE HOUSE LOCAL GOVERNMENT ENGROSSED BILL
Introduced by: Senator Rohl
1 An Act to limit the types of residential improvements for which counties,
2 municipalities, and townships may require a permit.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 11-10:
5 Notwithstanding any authority granted to a county, municipality, or township to
6 prescribe building codes or standards or to issue building permits, a county, municipality,
7 or township may not require a building permit for the repair or substantially similar
8 replacement of any of the following existing exterior parts of an a detached single-family
9 owner-occupied residential structure:
10 (1) Door, if the dimensions remain the same;
11 (2) Downspout;
12 (3) Fascia,
13 (4) Fencing panel or post;
14 (4)(5) Gutter;
15 (5)(6) Siding, if nonstructural;
16 (7) Soffit; and
17 (6)(8) Window, if the dimensions remain the same.
18 Nothing in this section relieves a homeowner from compliance with any other
19 applicable building code or ordinance.
20 A county, municipality, or township may require building permits for repairs
21 pursuant to this section or substantially similar replacements for exterior parts of any
22 designated historical property as authorized under chapter 1-19B.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
