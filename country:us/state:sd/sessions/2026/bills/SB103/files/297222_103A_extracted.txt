Section 1:
103A 101st Legislative Session 103
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 103A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Senator Carley
1 An Act to address the assumption of risk by an individual present at a shooting
2 range.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 20-9:
5 An individual present at a shooting range assumes all associated risks, to the extent
6 the risks are obvious and inherent. Those risks include injuries that may result from:
7 (1) Noise;
8 (2) The discharge of any projectile or shot;
9 (3) A malfunction of shooting equipment not owned by the sport shooting range;
10 (4) Natural variations in terrain;
11 (5) Surface or subsurface snow or ice conditions; and
12 (6) Bare spots, rocks, trees, and other forms of natural growth or debris.
13 For purposes of this section, a "shooting range" means an area designed and
14 operated for the use of pistols, rifles, or shotguns, as a means of silhouette, skeet, trap,
15 black powder, or other shooting, and archery. A shooting range may be located on public
16 or private land, and may be operated by a public or a private entity, and includes. The
17 term does not include a law enforcement shooting range.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
