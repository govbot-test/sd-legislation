Section 1:
198C 101st Legislative Session 198
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 198C
FOR THE SENATE EDUCATION ENGROSSED BILL
Introduced by: Senator Karr
1 An Act to restrict the use of a cell phone by a student during the school day.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 13-32:
4 Except as otherwise provided in this section, a student may not use a cell phone
5 during the school day, as set by the board of the school district pursuant to § 13-26-1.
6 A student may use a cell phone during the school day if the use is:
7 (1) The use of a cell phone is required or permitted as part of an approved medical or
8 educational accommodation; or Pre-approved on an individual student basis by an
9 administrator to manage the student's health care;
10 (2) The use of a cell phone is required Required or permitted as part of:
11 (a) A plan under section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act, 20 U.S.C. § 794 (January
12 1, 2026); or
13 (b) An individual education program under 20 U.S.C. § 1401 (January 1, 2026);
14 (3) Permitted by a teacher as part of the implementation of the teacher's lesson plan;
15 or
16 (4) Required during an emergency affecting the health, safety, or well-being of a
17 student or staff member.
18 The board of each school district shall adopt a policy that sets forth disciplinary
19 actions for student noncompliance.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
