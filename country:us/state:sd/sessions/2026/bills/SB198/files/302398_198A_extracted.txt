Section 1:
198A 101st Legislative Session 198
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 198A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Senator Karr
1 An Act to restrict the use of a cell phone by a student during instructional time the
2 school day.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 13-32:
5 Except as otherwise provided in this section, a student may not use a cell phone
6 during the school day, as set by the board of the school district pursuant to § 13-26-1.
7 A student may use a cell phone during the school day if:
8 (1) The use of a cell phone is required or permitted as part of an approved medical or
9 educational accommodation; or
10 (2) The use of a cell phone is required during an emergency affecting the health,
11 safety, or well-being of a student or staff member.
12 The board of each school district shall adopt a policy that sets forth disciplinary
13 actions for student noncompliance.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
