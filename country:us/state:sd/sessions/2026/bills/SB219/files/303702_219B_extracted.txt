Section 1:
219B 101st Legislative Session 219
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 219B
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Senator Hohn
1 An Act to require that a state agency consult with the Aeronautics Commission when
2 prior to purchasing, transferring, or disposing of a state owned and operated
3 aircraft.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. That § 50-2-1.1 be AMENDED:
6 50-2-1.1. The commission shall continue within the department, and all its
7 functions shall be performed by the department as provided by § 1-44-11.
8 The commission shall provide advice and expertise to a state agencies agency
9 regarding the purchase, transfer and disposition of state owned and operated aircraft,
10 including those owned or operated by any state institution. A state agency shall consult
11 the commission when prior to purchasing, transferring, or disposing of state owned and
12 operated aircraft, including excluding any unmanned aircraft, small unmanned aircraft,
13 and small unmanned aircraft system as defined in § 50-1-1.
14 The commission shall provide advice and expertise in the form of a written report
15 to the Governor and the agency requesting the information.
16 The requirements of this section do not apply to the Board of Regents or any
17 institutions of higher education under the control of the board, if the board determines
18 that the primary use of the aircraft is for educational purposes.
19
