Section 1:
1167A 101st Legislative Session 1167
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDME NT 1167A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Representative Aylward
1 An Act to revise a provision related to loitering within a community safety zone.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 22-24B-24 be AMENDED:
4 22-24B-24. No person who is required to register as a sex offender, as defined in
5 this chapter, may loiter within enter a community safety zone or a public library unless
6 the person, including for the purpose of observing or contacting a minor related to or in
7 the care and custody of the person.
8 This section does not apply if the offender was under the age of eighteen at the
9 time of the offense and the offender was not tried and convicted of the offense as an adult,
10 or the circuit court has entered an order pursuant to § 22-24B-28 exempting the offender
11 from the provisions of §§ 22-24B-22 to 22-24B-28, inclusive.
12 A violation of this section is a Class 6 felony. Any subsequent violation is a Class 5
13 felony.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
