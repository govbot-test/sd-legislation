Section 1:
45A 101st Legislative Session 45
2026 South Dakota Legislature
AMENDM ENT 45A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: The Chair of the Committee on Judiciary at the request of the Attorney General
1 An Act to revise a provision regulating delta-8 tetrahydrocannabinol, THC-O acetate,
2 and hexahydrocannabinol for persons under the age of twenty-one and to
3 provide a penalty therefor.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. That § 34-20B-117 be AMENDED:
6 34-20B-117. The following actions are unlawful:
7 (1) To knowingly sell or distribute a product intended for human consumption
8 containing delta-8 tetrahydrocannabinol, delta-9 tetrahydrocannabinolic acid, THC-
9 O acetate, or hexahydrocannabinol to a person under the age of twenty-one, unless
10 the substance was obtained directly or pursuant to a valid prescription or order
11 from a practitioner, acting in the course of the practitioner's professional practice;
12 (2) The purchase or attempt to purchase, the receipt or attempt to receive, the
13 possession, or the consumption of, a product intended for human consumption
14 containing delta-8 tetrahydrocannabinol, delta-9 tetrahydrocannabinolic acid, THC-
15 O acetate, or hexahydrocannabinol to by a person under the age of twenty-one,
16 unless the substance was obtained directly or pursuant to a valid prescription or
17 order from a practitioner, acting in the course of the practitioner's professional
18 practice; and
19 (3) To purchase a product intended for human consumption containing delta-8
20 tetrahydrocannabinol, delta-9 tetrahydrocannabinolic acid, THC-O acetate, or
21 hexahydrocannabinol on behalf of, or to give a product intended for human
22 consumption containing delta-8 tetrahydrocannabinol, delta-9
23 tetrahydrocannabinolic acid, THC-O acetate, or hexahydrocannabinol to, any
24 person under the age of twenty-one, unless the:
25 (a) The purchaser is a parent or guardian of the person under the age of
26 twenty-one; and
